# Appendix AS – Job Class Code Long

:::note
Appendix AS is for use with Field 44 (JOB CLASS CODE LONG) in the Staff Template
:::

:::note
Tenure is the status of being a “professional employee”. Temporary professional employees attain tenure, and become professional employees, upon successful completion of three-years of successful practice as a temporary professional in accordance with 24 P.S. § 1108.*
:::

*\*Act 13 guidance for evaluation of Professional Employees can be found here:* [Act 13 Educator Effectiveness Toolkit](https://pdesas.org/Frameworks/DCEToolKit/Act13EvaluationOfEducatorEffectivenessToolkit)

:::warning[Disclaimer:] 
The codes and definitions used in this document do not represent legal opinions. For questions regarding the employment status of individual employees, LEAs are directed to consult with their solicitor or other qualified counsel.
:::
