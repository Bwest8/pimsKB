The codes listed below are only to be used for Field 6 (ENROLLMENT CODE) in the School Enrollment Template. These codes do not replace any existing codes that may be native to your local Student Information System (SIS). Please continue to use enrollment codes in your SIS which support other purposes, such as Child Accounting aggregate calculations, scheduling changes, room changes, etc. The codes listed in the table below are the only codes that will be accepted in the School Enrollment Template submission to PIMS.
