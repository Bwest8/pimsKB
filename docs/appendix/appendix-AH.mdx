| Job Description Code | Role                         | Definition                                                                                                                                              | Example                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
|----------------------|------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| `SWS`                  | School and Student Access    | Access to PVAAS as a School User for school level reports and student level reports (including value-added, diagnostic, and student projection reports) | This often includes lead teachers, classroom teachers, and other professional staff who need access to school level data AND student level data such as, but not limited to: Asst Principal, Dean of Students, School Counselor, regular and special education teachers responsible for instruction in state tested grades/subjects/courses, and teachers responsible for interventions/academic supports for students. |
| `SNS`                  | School and No Student Access | Access to PVAAS as a School User for school level reports (including value-added and diagnostic reports), but NO access to student data                 | This often includes classroom teachers and other professional staff who need access to school level data but do NOT need access to student level data.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| `NPA`                  | No PVAAS Access              | No access to PVAAS as a School User                                                                                                                     | This staff member does NOT need a School User PVAAS account. This could include school staff who do not need access to PVAAS, as well as district administrators who would access PVAAS as a District Users or Admin.                                                                                                                                                                                                   |

:::note
Access to student-level data is determined locally by each LEA and should follow local policies regarding data access. The Job Description Codes above apply only to PVAAS School User accounts/access. Admin-type accounts, District User accounts, and access customizations must be managed manually in the PVAAS system by the appropriate users.
:::
